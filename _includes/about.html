About Dr. Stark

Dr. Henry K Stark is a Podiatry Specialist who returned to practice October, 2020, to Lake Park, Florida. Dr. Stark graduated with honors from New York College Of Podiatric Medicine in 1978. He then trained for two more years in the Pittsburgh area, learning extensive, post-graduate surgical techniques. Dr. Stark came to Lake Park to begin his practice carreer as an associate in 1980. Six years later, he opened his own practice in as a Podiatrist, Foot and Ankle Surgeon, hiring associates and forming a partnership, eventually expanding to four offices in the Northern half of Palm Beach County. After selling his practice in 2015, Dr. Stark had retired after actively practicing extensively and on staff at nine area hospitals, including Jupiter medical center, Palm Beach Gardens Medical Center, Good Samaritan Medical Center, St Mary's Medical Center, JFK Medical Centers amoung others. He has now returned to his original office at 701 Park Avenue, Lake Park, reopening as Lake Park Podiatry. Dr. Stark's goals is to provide his patients the most modern podiatry but with that old-fashioned care.

About Dr. Stark

Dr. Henry K Stark is a Podiatry Specialist wwho has returned to practice in Lake Park, Florida. He graduated with honors from New York College Of Podiatric Medicine in 1978. Dr. Stark spent two years in the Pittsburgh area in his extensive, post-graduate surgical training. He began practice in Lake Park in 1980 as an associate. Six years later, Dr. Stark opened his own practice in as a Podiatrist, Foot and Ankle Surgeon, expanding to four offices in the Northern half of Palm Beach County. After selling his practice in 2015, Dr. Stark had retired after actively practicing extensively and on staff at nine area hospitals, including Palm Beach Gardens Medical Center, JFK Hospitals, Jupiter Medical Center, Good Samaritan Medical Center, St Mary's Medical Center. He has returned to his original office at 701 Park Avenue, Lake Park, and has reopened as Lake Park Podiatry. His goal is to provide his patients the most modern podiatry but with that old-fashioned care. Call Dr. Henry K Stark ({{site.phone}}) for more information and advice or to book an appointment.
